Electronics Recycling Guide

Learn how to properly recycle your electronic devices and contribute to a sustainable future.

How to Recycle Your Electronics

  1. Identify Recyclable Electronics

    Common recyclable items include computers, smartphones, tablets, printers, and televisions.

  2. Remove Personal Data

    Ensure all personal information is wiped from your devices before recycling.

  3. Remove Batteries

    If possible, remove batteries from devices as they may need to be recycled separately.

  4. Package Safely

    Pack your electronics securely to prevent damage during transportation.

  5. Schedule a Pickup or Drop-off

    Use our pickup service or find a nearby recycling center to dispose of your electronics.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is electronics recycling important?

Electronics recycling helps conserve natural resources, reduces pollution, and prevents hazardous materials from entering landfills.

What happens to my recycled electronics?

Recycled electronics are dismantled, and their components are sorted into materials that can be reused in new products or safely disposed of.

Is there a fee for recycling electronics?

Most of our recycling services are free. However, there may be a small fee for certain large items or bulk recycling.
