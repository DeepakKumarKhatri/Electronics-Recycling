About EcoTech Recycle

Our Mission

At EcoTech Recycle, we are committed to creating a sustainable future by promoting responsible electronics recycling. Our mission is to reduce e-waste, conserve natural resources, and protect the environment while providing convenient and rewarding recycling solutions for individuals and businesses.

Our Story

EcoTech Recycle was founded in 2015 by environmental engineer Sarah Johnson. After witnessing the growing problem of electronic waste in landfills, Sarah was inspired to create a solution that would make electronics recycling accessible and rewarding for everyone. What started as a small local initiative has now grown into a nationwide network of recycling centers and partnerships.

Our Recycling Process

  1. Collection

    We collect electronics through our convenient pickup service or at our drop-off locations.

  2. Sorting

    Devices are sorted by type and condition to determine the best recycling method.

  3. Data Destruction

    All data-containing devices undergo secure data wiping or physical destruction.

  4. Dismantling

    Devices are carefully disassembled to separate different components and materials.

  5. Material Recovery

    Valuable materials are extracted and processed for reuse in new products.

  6. Responsible Disposal

    Any remaining materials are disposed of in an environmentally friendly manner.
